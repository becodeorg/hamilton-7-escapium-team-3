Who Should Play ?

Escapium is perfect for everyone. There are no special skills needed to have a great time. Bring your friends, family, co-worker, just a bunch of random people and get fun.

For Team Building

Our escape rooms are designed for intense participation from the group trying to escape. It's perfect for team building experience in your city.

For Special Events

Whatever the celebration Escapium is here to take it to another level. Book your room and contact us to see how we can help make any special occasion even better
